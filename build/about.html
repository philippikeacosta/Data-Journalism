What is the photography of New York?

It's any photography taken of or in the city! That includes photos of buildings, parks, people, major events in New York, or any other scene.

Why am I interested in it?

I've been taking photography at school for several years now, and I really love it. I've also lived in New York for my entire life, so I wanted to explore the intersection between the two!
